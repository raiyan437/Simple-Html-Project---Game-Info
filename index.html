PC games, also known as personal computer games, are interactive software designed to run on personal computers that allow users to engage in various forms of gameplay. The genre of PC games can vary widely, ranging from action and adventure games, to strategy and simulation games, to sports and racing games, and many others. PC games have evolved greatly over the years, with advances in technology leading to improved graphics, more realistic sound effects, and more immersive gameplay. The popularity of PC games continues to grow, with millions of people around the world enjoying these games for entertainment and leisure purposes.
